There are Different Types of Skateboards
 
 
The five main types of skateboards are the Trick board, the Longboard, the Penny board, Cruisers and the Mountain board
 
 

The
Trick board


The Trick board is a medium sized board with hard rubber/plastic wheels, a tail on the front and back with sand paper on top so you can flick with your foot..

The
Longboard


The Longboard is a board that usually has thick rubber wheels and usually has no tail, this board is for going fast.

The
Penny Board

The Penny board is a small board that is usually made with colored plastic and has a tail and thick rubber wheels.

The
Cruisers


Cruisers are shorter longboards with thick wheels, it has a tail more often than the longboard.

The
Mountain Boards

The Mountain board is a 'offroading' board that has thick inflating wheels like a mountain bike, usually these boards have straps for your feet making it harder to fall out.
